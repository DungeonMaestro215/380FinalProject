Hello! I'm Will and I'm a third-yeah at UNC majoring in computer science and minoring in geology. It rocks B). I found out about Raspberry Pi's at the beginning of college when I was looking into more ways of getting into computer science. I heard nothing but good things about them so I decided to buy one...that was my best decision ever.
