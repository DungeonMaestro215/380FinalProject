Overview

Raspberry Pi's Are Cool

This website covers a general overview of the Raspberry Pi single-board computer. We will look into the history of the Raspberry Pi, the technical specifications of the computer, and some things people use the Pi for, including our own projects. Why is the Raspberry Pi so popular? What makes the newer models different/better than the old models? Why is it such a good development platform?
